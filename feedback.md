## Project #1: Simple and learner-friendly vocabulary support

### Strength Keywords
vocabulary reinforcement · repetition · pronunciation practice · sound-picture-meaning connection · visual support · classroom applicability · retry design · level differentiation

### Feedback Summary
Project #1 was consistently recognized for its strong practical value in vocabulary learning, particularly in supporting repeated exposure, individualized review, and multimodal engagement through sound, meaning, and visual cues. Peers appreciated how naturally the app addresses a real classroom challenge, namely providing meaningful vocabulary reinforcement beyond limited class time. Its intuitive design and immediate classroom applicability were viewed as major strengths, while suggestions such as adaptive feedback, selective visual support, and richer retry cycles point to promising directions for further refinement into an even more interactive learner-centered tool.

### Peer feedback radar profile
Project #1 demonstrates balanced strength across all evaluation areas, particularly in educational usefulness, pedagogical thinking, and clear app design. Its strongest quality lies in translating a simple learning idea into an accessible and instructionally meaningful tool that could be readily adopted in real classroom settings. For future improvement, adding more adaptive feedback features and richer learner interaction could further deepen engagement while preserving its simplicity and usability.
